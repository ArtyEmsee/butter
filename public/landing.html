What is Churning?

Churning is the art of signing up for credit cards to get their sign-up bonuses and then cancelling them. People churn credit cards to get free flights, free nights at hotels, cash back, or other benefits.

What does Butter do?

Monitor your credit cards

Add your churning related credit card information to track how close you are to meeting minimum spend on a card for that awesome sign-up bonus. Enter the date you want to cancel cards as a reminder to avoid paying annual fees.

Track Your Rewards Points

See how many Hotel points, General points, Miles, or how much Cash Back you've earned to get a sense of where you're earning the most. View how many points you've earned by reward program and easily navigate to that program's rewards portal to redeem your points.

Manufacture Spend

Need to meet minimum spend on a card in a pinch? Easily purchase Amazon eGift Cards or any other Amazon product from our Amazon page. Search for what you need and click to navigate directly to that product's page on Amazon.
